Consider a Gaussian model $Y \mid f(\theta) \sim N( f(\theta), I)$. 
Given an observation $y$, the negative log-likelihood is then

$$
\ell(\theta) = -\log p(y, \theta) = \frac{1}{2} \| y - f(\theta) \|^2 + \text{const}. 
$$
