Consider GF for a real-analytic objective function $L(\theta)$. 

<br>

By Theorem 2.2 in [1](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/10.1137/040605266), either $\theta_t$ goes unbounded, or it converges to a critical point. (In many cases, boundedness can be obtained via conservation laws.)
